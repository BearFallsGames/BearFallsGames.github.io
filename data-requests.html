Deleting your Data

In order to delete or receive a copy of your data, please email bearfallsgames@gmail.com with a subject of "Personal Data Deletion Request" or "Personal Data View Request" and your Google Advertising ID, which can be found in the following location on your android device

Settings > Google > Ads

If you do not have an advertising ID, you have already opted out of data collection and we have respected that signal. Submitted requests will be forwarded to our service providers, where they will be serviced within 30 days unless otherwise legally required. For deletion requests, we will forward confirmation that your device ID has been removed once it is received. View requests will be given a response containing the requested data.
